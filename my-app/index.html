The Problem

We've all paid huge amounts of money for cab rides. It is only logical to think that the drivers earn at least par income and are able to live a healthy lifestyle. However this notion is completely false, thanks to corporate greed. During financial crises, one would expect companies to support their workers to the fullest and help them when they most need it. Ironically, cab booking companies do the exact opposite by overcharging the users and keeping the profits to themselves, thus exploiting the drivers and misusing the end-user's money

The Mission

In an ideal world, everyone wins and no one goes home feeling sad. Though we can't create a Utopia we can at least strive to change the system in such a way that no one gets exploited and there is a transparent and honest way of distributing profits. If there is a loss, everyone loses something and not just the workers.

The Solution

How does one guarantee transparency and give ownership to all the contributors? This is where blockchain technology comes in. The entire exploitation problem can be solved by making a DAO instead of a regular company. Smart contracts will have algorithms to deal with fluctuations in prices and all the workers will have some percentage of ownership of the DAO. This will ensure that they have a say in the DAO's decision making process
